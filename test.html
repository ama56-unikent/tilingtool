Tiles

How to use

Use the Slicer to create tiles. Adjust the crosshairs using the mouse. When ready to slice, hold the H key and click the left mouse button to slice the canvas horizontally or hold V to slice it vertically. You can also hold both keys simultaneously to perform a double slice.

Use the Resizer to resize existing tiles. Hover over any of the margin areas for the resize cursor to appear; then, hold the left mouse button and drag your mouse to adjust the tile. You can do this both vertically and horizontally.

When happy with your grid, click on Code View and then copy the markup.

Have fun tiling! ☺
